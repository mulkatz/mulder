# Letters to the Editor: Reader Sightings Roundup

## Triangular Craft Over Lake Michigan
*Submitted by Robert Kowalski, Muskegon, MI*

On the night of November 12, 2023, I observed a triangular craft with white lights at each vertex hovering silently over Lake Michigan. The object remained stationary for approximately seven minutes before accelerating eastward at tremendous speed. Two other witnesses on the pier corroborated my account.

## Strange Lights in the Black Forest
*Submitted by Ingrid Hoffmann, Freiburg, Germany*

During a hiking trip in the Black Forest on August 3, 2024, my husband and I observed three orange orbs moving in perfect formation above the treeline. The objects pulsated with a rhythmic pattern and made no sound. We managed to capture a brief video on my phone, which has been submitted to MUFON for analysis.

## Editor's Response

We thank our readers for these detailed accounts. Both sightings have been forwarded to our investigation team for follow-up. We encourage all witnesses to file official reports through the established channels.
